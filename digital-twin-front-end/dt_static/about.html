"Used to observe and improve the productiveness of the object
through simulations and real-time data acquisition." ​


This Digital Twin is developed by UoM's 2020-2021 Team 1 for the final year MEng project.
Equipped with sensors that send data in real-time, the induction motor in UoM's Sackville Street Building sends data to the digital model
which displays key aspects of the machine’s behaviour and used as an input for MATLAB models used for predictive maintenance.
